Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie Recipes

Introducing the perfect smoothie!

Following this recipes will turn you to a better smoothie maker.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the banana, peanut butter, and milk.
  2. Add honey if desired, and ice cubes for a colder smoothie.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
