Scrambled Egg Recipes

Introducing the perfect scrambled egg!

Following this recipes will turn you to a better egg cooker.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them together with milk (if using), salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Let the eggs sit for a few moments, then gently stir with a spatula.
  5. Cook until the eggs are softly set, then serve immediately.
